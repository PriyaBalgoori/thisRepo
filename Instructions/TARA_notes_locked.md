## What TARA is
"The star — the 50 names ranking highest on the SS360 Composite among stocks
CURRENTLY IN TWR ALIGNMENT." (as of a date, e.g. 2026-07-07, "50 currently-trading stars").

It is a TWO-STAGE FILTER, not a signal:
1. GATE: keep only stocks currently in TWR alignment AND Tide-bull (three-lights, long side).
2. RANK: of those, take the 50 highest on the SS360 Composite = RS · EPS · Acc/Dis
   (relative strength · earnings · accumulation-distribution).
   "A ranking, not a recommendation or forecast."

## Why it is DIFFERENT from what we tested (and possibly better)
- Everything we tested = single-name intraday SIGNAL TIMING (zone crossings). Coin flip x3.
- TARA's claim is NOT "aligned names go up." It is "aligned AND fundamentally strong AND
  under accumulation AND in a confirmed uptrend names are worth holding."
- The alignment is just the GATE; the edge (if any) lives in the COMPOSITE RANK (RS/EPS/Acc-Dis).
- Our intraday logs have NONE of that (no RS, no EPS, no Acc/Dis) -> we literally could not
  have tested TARA. The coin-flip results DO NOT touch TARA's thesis.
- TARA ~ CAN SLIM / relative-strength momentum + quality + trend. That factor combo has
  DECADES of out-of-sample academic + practitioner support. It is the PLAUSIBLE one.
